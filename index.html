Designed an advanced optimization model to maximize Fantasy Premier League points. Using Python, pandas, and sasoptpy, I developed and solved a multi-period optimization problem, ensuring team selection and budget constraints were adhered to at each stage. The model generates optimal team picks and strategies on a weekly basis, offering data-backed insights for high performance.

Cable Failure Analysis Project

Led a group project focused on analyzing cable failure data to model the relationships between cycles until failure and various influencing variables, including cable length, movement amplitude, and load weight. Utilized statistical transformations and models in R to enhance predictive accuracy, conducting rigorous cross-validation to ensure the robustness of the model performance. This project culminated in a detailed presentation and poster, showcasing our findings. Through this experience, I strengthened my leadership, statistical analysis, and presentation skills, demonstrating my ability to drive a project from conception to completion.

Numerical Polynomial Interpolation Project

Created and implemented Python algorithms for uniform, non-uniform, and piecewise polynomial interpolation using Lagrange polynomials. The project involved rigorous error analysis and validation, ensuring high accuracy and reliability in interpolations. This project achieved 87.5% and demonstrated an advanced understanding of numerical accuracy.
